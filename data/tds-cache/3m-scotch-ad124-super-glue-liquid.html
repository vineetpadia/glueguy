Scotch® Super Glue Liquid in Precision Applicator features patented droplet control technology for the ultimate in precision dispensing. The clog resistant "stay fresh" bottle avoids dry out, so you can use the bottle over and over again. Use on wood, metal, ceramic, leather, rubber, vinyl, some plastics, and more.

Installation **IMPORTANT**: The 3M‚ĄĘ Scotchcast‚ĄĘ Connector Sealing Pack 3570G-N should remain sealed in the guard bag (white aluminized bag) until ready to use. In cold weather, warm closed mixing pouch to 50¬įF (10¬įC) or warmer prior to mixing. Keep in a warm area, such as truck cab or inside pocket, until ready to use.

1. Thoroughly clean and dry the surface of the substrate to which the material is desired to bond.
2. Remove guard bag, using caution not to damage inner bag.
3. Grip both edges of bag at the center barrier (fig. 1) and wrinkle and flex the bag across the barrier. This will weaken the barrier.
4. Squeeze the clear side of the resin, forcing the resin through the center barrier.
5. Mix thoroughly to a uniform color by squeezing contents back and forth 25-30 times.
6. Squeeze resin to one end of bag and cut off other end. (fig. 2)

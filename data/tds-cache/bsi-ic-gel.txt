It has the same bonding and curing time characteristics as MAXI-CURE&#8482;. IC-GEL&#8482;, however, can be applied to a vertical surface and will stay in place. It will not run. This can be very convenient for some assembly applications. Applying CA to the bottom of a horizontal surface, such as a ceiling, can be very difficult with anything other than a full standard CA bottle. IC-GEL&#8482; can be applied at any angle with just a squeeze of the tube. The gel does, however, have a tendency to continue to come out of the tube for about a second after pressure is released, so this must be taken into account to apply the exact amount of IC-GEL&#8482; that you want. IC-GEL&#8482; is an excellent putty for plastic models. It will fill any void and can be formed to many shapes. Applying INSTA-SET&#8482; allows IC-GEL&#8482; to be sanded or filed to final shape in less than 20 seconds. Autobody repairmen have finished their jobs in a fraction of the time by using IC-GEL&#8482; with the additional advantage over normal body putties of superior bonding to metal surfaces. IC-GEL's&#8482; most popular application is for the bonding of fragments of live coral (frags) to underwater rocks in aquariums. It has proven to be non-toxic to aquatic organisms and provides instant bonding for the propagation of the coral. IC-Gel has a superior shelf life of over three years and its application nozzle makes it the easiest of all CAs to apply. Click to view Long Island Aquarium Coral Reef Video .

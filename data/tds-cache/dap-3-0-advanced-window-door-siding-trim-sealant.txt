# DAP® 3.0™ High Performance Window, Door, Siding &amp; Trim Sealant

## PRODUCT DESCRIPTION

DAP® 3.0™ HIGH PERFORMANCE WINDOW, DOOR, SIDING &amp; TRIM SEALANT offers superior flexibility and adhesion for a durable, 100% waterproof and weatherproof seal that won't crack or shrink. It stays flexible to withstand expansion and contraction caused by temperature and weather fluctuations. It can be applied in extreme temperatures: 20°F to 120°F and adheres to wet/damp surfaces. DAP 3.0 guns out easily and tools smoothly. It is rain and paint ready in just 30 minutes, saving time. Once cured, the sealant is mold and mildew resistant. It is low in odor and VOC compliant. Backed by a Lifetime Guarantee. Meets ASTM C920, Class 35, and complies with AAMA 808.3-16 Type I, AAMA 803.3-16 Type I, and AAMA 802.2-16 Type II. Interior/exterior use.

# FOR BEST RESULTS
- Application temperature range is between 20°F and 120°F.
- Joint width should not exceed ½". If joint depth exceeds ½", use foam backer rod.
- Not recommended for continuous underwater use, high temperature surfaces or for surface defects.
- Certain porous substrates, such as concrete, may require primer for best adhesion.
- Store below 80°F in dry place for optimal shelf life.

# APPLICATION

## Surface Preparation
Surface must be clean, dry, structurally sound and free of old caulk, dirt, dust &amp; other foreign material.

## Product Application
1. Cut nozzle at a 45° angle to desired bead size.
2. Puncture inner foil seal.
3. Load into caulk gun.
4. Fill gap or crack with sealant.

# CLEAN UP &amp; STORAGE

Remove excess uncured sealant from surfaces and tools with mineral spirits. Excess cured sealant must be cut or scraped away. Do not use mineral spirits to clean hands or skin. Wash hands or skin with soap and water. Store container in temperatures below 80°F and in a dry place.

Product: DOWSIL EA-9187L UV Silicone Conformal Coating. Form No. 11-3909-01, 2018. Description: one-part medium-viscosity conformal coating that is UL approved. Features: controlled volatile content to avoid electric failure; no heating, no exothermic heat during cure, no solvents; excellent unprimed adhesion to many substrates; faster in-line processing at room temperature with optional heat acceleration; UV indicator; UL 746C and UL 94 V-0 specification.

Applications and methods: flowable one-component neutral-cure material designed for manufacture of electrical and electronic products and components requiring high reliability, UL 746C and UL 94 V-0. Application methods: spray, brush, flow, automated pattern coating and dip coating with special precautions.

Typical properties from the TDS: one part; appearance translucent; viscosity 900 mPa.s; skin-over time at 25 C 9 min; specific gravity cured 0.98; durometer 17 Shore A; tensile strength 0.44 MPa; elongation 165%; unprimed lap shear on glass 0.31 MPa; dielectric strength 20 kV/mm; volume resistivity 3E+15 ohm.cm; dielectric constant at 1 MHz 2.8; dissipation factor at 1 MHz 0.0009; UL flammability classification 94 V-0; agency listing UL 746C; storage condition 0-30 C; shelf life 360 days.

Processing and cure: solventless RTV elastomeric conformal coatings require atmospheric moisture to cure. Cure depends on application method, film thickness, temperature and humidity. Skin-over time is a non-tacky-surface indication; full cure times develop full physical properties such as durometer, tensile strength or adhesion and can be improved with mild heat of 60 C or less. Pot life depends on application method; minimize ambient moisture exposure with dry air or dry nitrogen blanketing when possible.

Adhesion and repair: adhesion can lag behind cure and may take up to 72 h at room temperature to build; test adhesion after 72 h. Apply to clean and dry substrates. Difficult low-surface-energy surfaces may need priming or chemical/plasma etching. The coating can be removed by scraping, cutting, solvents or stripping agents. A soldering iron may be applied through the coating to replace a component with proper ventilation, followed by cleaning, drying and recoating.

Temperature, storage and limitations: Dow states most silicone adhesives are operational from -45 to 200 C for long periods, with possible thermal cycling to -55 C if verified on the user's assembly. Keep containers tightly closed, minimize headspace, purge partially filled containers with dry air or nitrogen, and prevent moisture contact. Multiple packaging sizes are available. Not tested or represented for medical or pharmaceutical uses. Typical values are not for specification writing.

# PRODUCT DESCRIPTION

Fabri-Fuse allows exceptional adhesion on fabric to fabric or decorative items to fabric. Its extreme flexibility will allow fabric to remain flexible – even after washing and can be sewn through once dry!

# WHERE TO USE / APPLICATIONS

Adhere appliques, patches, trims, lace, ribbon and decorative accents. Works great on fabric, felt, yarn, metal, wood, glass, ceramic, Styrofoam™, vinyl, rubber and much more!

# DIRECTIONS and APPLICATION NOTES

Always test a small area before full use.

Step 1 Remove inner seal.
Step 2 Apply directly to each surface to be bonded.
Step 3 Mate surfaces.
Step 4 Dry flat 8 hours.
Step 5 Washable after 72 hours.

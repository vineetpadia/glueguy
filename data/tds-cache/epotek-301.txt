Product: EPO-TEK 301, Spectrally Transparent Epoxy. Date August 2024, Rev XIV. No. of components: two. Mix ratio by weight: 20:5. Specific gravity: Part A 1.15, Part B 0.87. Pot life: 1-2 hours. Shelf life bulk: one year at room temperature. Recommended cure: 65 C / 2 hours. Minimum alternative cures, which may not achieve listed performance: 65 C / 1 hour or 23 C / 24 hours.

Notes: containers should be kept closed when not in use. Filled systems should be stirred thoroughly before mixing and prior to use. Performance properties including rheology and conductivity may vary when bi-pak or syringe packaging or post-processing is performed. Syringe packaging can impact initial viscosity and effective pot life. Total mass should not exceed 25 grams.

Product description: two-component, room-temperature-curing epoxy featuring very low viscosity and excellent optical-mechanical properties. Physical properties: color before cure Part A clear/colorless, Part B clear/colorless. Consistency pourable liquid. Viscosity at 23 C and 100 rpm is 100-200 cP. Thixotropic index N/A. Glass transition temperature >=65 C. Coefficient of thermal expansion below Tg 39 x 10^-6 in/in C and above Tg 98 x 10^-6 in/in C. Shore D hardness 85. Lap shear at 23 C >2,000 psi. Die shear at 23 C >=10 kg and 3,556 psi. Degradation temperature 430 C. Weight loss: 0.12% at 200 C, 0.13% at 250 C, 0.39% at 300 C. Suggested operating temperature <300 C intermittent. Storage modulus 436,249 psi. Particle size N/A.

Electrical and thermal properties: thermal conductivity N/A. Volume resistivity at 23 C >=1 x 10^13 ohm-cm. Dielectric constant at 1 kHz 4.00. Dissipation factor at 1 kHz 0.016.

Optical properties at 23 C: spectral transmission >=99% at 382-980 nm, >=97% at 980-1,640 nm, >=95% at 1,640-2,040 nm. Refractive index 1.519 at 589 nm.

Suggested application notes: semiconductor optical glob top or underfill; adhesion to common wafer passivation, solder mask and flex circuits; compatible with LED die, Si and GaAs. PCB general potting and protection over FR4, flex or ceramic PCBs. Fiber optic adhesive for glass and plastic fibers, wicking into fiber bundles used in patch cords or sensor devices, adhesive/seal/encapsulant for fiber packaging and components, transmission of IR up to 2500 nm, terminating fibers into ferrules, fiber coupling and splicing. Optoelectronic LCD/LED adhesive for laminating glass layers, adhesion to PET plastic, general potting, encapsulation and protection, spectral transmission in VIS and IR light, adhesive/encapsulant for VCSEL packaged devices, resisting yellowing per ASTM D1925, adhesive for precision optics including lens, prism, beam splitter cubes, mirrors and diodes. NASA approved low-outgassing epoxy. For ISO 10993 biocompatible version see EPO-TEK MED-301.

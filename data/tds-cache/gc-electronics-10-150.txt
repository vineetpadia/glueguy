Product: 10-150 RTV Silicone Rubber Adhesive Sealant, 3 oz tube with dispensing nozzle. Description: clear one-component room-temperature-vulcanizing silicone rubber adhesive/sealant. Uses: general electrical insulation, potting exposed electronic components, bonding gaskets for heating/refrigeration units, formed-in-place gaskets for gear boxes, compressors, pumps and outdoor motor covers, pressure sealing aircraft cabins and cockpits, caulking sheet metal stacks, ductwork and equipment housings, and anti-abrasion coatings. Cure/handling: cures to a tough rubbery solid on exposure to atmospheric moisture; tack-free in 10 minutes; full cure in 24 hours. Temperature: catalog states service from sub-zero to 400 F; 400 F equals about 204 C, but no exact lower numeric bound is published. Electrical: dielectric strength 635 V/mil / 25 kV/mm; volume resistivity 6 x 10^14 Ohm.cm; dielectric constant 2.8 at 60 Hz, 100 Hz and 100 kHz; dissipation factor 0.0015 at 60 Hz, 100 Hz and 100 kHz. Standards/compliance: FDA 21 CFR 175.105 when fully cured and washed, UL recognized for service to 150 C where elongation is not necessary, MIL-A-46106A Type 1, TT-S-001543A Class B, TT-S-0230C Type 2 Class B. Published source does not state tensile strength, elongation, hardness, lap shear, exact lower service temperature, thermal conductivity or a full physical-property table.

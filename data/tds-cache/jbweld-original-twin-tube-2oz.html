Specifications
Product Details

J-B Weld is the Original Cold Weld. It is a two-part epoxy system that creates strong, lasting repairs. With a 1:1 mixing ratio, it forms a permanent bond that can be shaped, tapped, filed, sanded, and drilled after curing. It sets in 4-6 hours to a dark gray color and fully cures in 15-24 hours for a hard bond. This product boasts a tensile strength of 6220 PSI and can withstand temperatures up to 550°F once fully cured.

Prepare

Clean surface area of dirt, grease, oil, paint, or loose debris. For best results use a detergent or degreaser to first clean the surface, then roughen surface with file or coarse sandpaper to provide the best repair.

Mix

Squeeze equal parts from each tube onto a disposable surface and mix thoroughly.

Apply

Apply with appropriate tool in an even coat, weld bead or extruded shape as needed.

Dry

Sets in 4-6 hours. Cures in 15-24 hours. Allow 4-6 hours before handling and 15 hours (minimum) before putting object back in use.

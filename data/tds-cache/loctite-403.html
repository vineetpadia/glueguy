TDS Download
A 1-part, low-odor, low-bloom, medium-viscosity instant adhesive ideal for aesthetic applications.
LOCTITE® 403 is a low-odor, low-bloom instant adhesive. This medium viscosity adhesive is ideal for applications where odor and/or blooming are a concern. LOCTITE 403 provides for rapid bonding of metals, elastomers and plastics, including difficult-to-bond plastics (PP, PE, PTFE) when used in conjunction with a primer such as LOCTITE® SF 770.
  • Low-odor for applications where vapor control is vital
  • Low-bloom for cosmetic applications
  • Medium viscosity for gaps of approximately 0.20 mm (0.008")
  • Bonds plastics, rubbers and metals

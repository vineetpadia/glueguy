Directions for Use
      1.     Dual cartridges:
             a) Insert the cartridge into the application gun
             and guide the plunger into the cartridge.
             b) Remove the cartridge cap and dispense
             material until both sides are flowing.
             c) Attach the static mixer to the end of the
             cartridge and begin dispensing the material.
             Ensure product is fully mixed (grey with no
             streaks).
      2.     Apply material to one of the substrates.
      3.     Join the parts. Parts must be joined within 2
             hours of mixing the two epoxy components.
      4.     Large quantities and/or higher temperature will
             decrease the usable life or pot life.
      5.     Apply pressure to the assembly by clamping for 8
             hours or until handling strength is obtained.
      6.     Full cure will be obtained after 7 days at 23°C
             (73°F).

Plexus MA425

SHARE THIS Share this content

  • Opens in a new window

Description

Two-part methacrylate adhesive designed for structural bonding of composite, thermoplastic, and metal assemblies. Plexus MA425 is finding wide usage for composite bonding applications in the transportation and marine industries because it requires virtually no surface preparation.

Key Features

  • Working time of 30 – 35 mins
  • Exceptional strength, elongation and fatigue resistance
  • Non-sag thixotropic dispensing

Key Substrates

  • Composites DCPD, FRP, Gelcoat, Phenolics, Epoxy Based Composites, RIM
  • Thermoplastics ABS/ ABS Blends, Acrylics, PBT, Polycarbonate, Rigid PVC, TPUs
  • Coated Metals Powder coated or E-coated Metals
  • Raw Metals with PC120 Aluminum, Stainless Steel, Raw Steel, etc.

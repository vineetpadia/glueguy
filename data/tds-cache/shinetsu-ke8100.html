High adhesion strength one-component heat cure RTV silicone rubber

KE-8100

KE-8100 is elastic adhesive that has higher bonding strength than conventional silicone adhesive.

  • Tensile lap-shear strength is 4.0 MPa
  • Easy to handle
  • Excellent heat, cold, weather resistance and electric insulation

Applications

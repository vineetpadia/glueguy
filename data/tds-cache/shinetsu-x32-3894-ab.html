CIPG (Cured In Place Gasket) Materials

X-32-3894-A/B

CIPG materials, which are liquid gaskets, allow the opening and closing of lids and repair the component inside.
Even after opening and closing the lids, CIPG materials sustain properties of gaskets.

Features and Benefits

  • Low compression set RTV silicone rubber ⇒
    It is possible to use as a gasket. Applicator can open packages and repair its components
  • Excellent adhesive properties with PBT ⇒ It is applicable to various substrates
  • Standard curing conditions  110°C × 30 min ⇒ Relatively low cure temperatures as addition cure type

Application Examples

  • Gaskets for electrical component packages

# TB1324 – ANAEROBIC SEALANT

## Information

TB1324 is a medium strength type anaerobic sealant. The performance characteristics of TB1324 include quick curing at room temperature, durability, sealing, and anti-corrosion.

## Application

Used for threaded fasteners with a small diameter that may be removed.

Elastic Bonding Adhesives
ThreeBonds’ industrial elastic adhesives’ major components are special polymer (non-solvent-based and moisture curable) and synthetic rubber (solvent-based).). They have good initial adhesion and will not lose their elasticity after bonding. No primer is needed and bonding to hard to bond substrates can be achieved.

With several different grades of elastic and bonding adhesives options, we ensure there is something for every application. Many of our rubber and polymer adhesives can be used for a variety of general purposes that make them ideal for a wide variety of industries, including: Automotive aftermarket, Automotive manufacturing, Consumer electronics, EV and fuel cell manufacturing, Motorsports manufacturing, and Small engine manufacturing.

How can you decide on the right elastic and bonding adhesives for your application? Our team can help. We offer detailed information about each one of our bonding adhesives so you can find the suitable appearance, viscosity, lap shear strength, hardness, and cure type to suit your manufacturing needs. We know there is no one-size-fits-all solution for our clients, so we go out of our way to customize our bonding adhesives to meet the criteria of various key applications. Ultimately, our goal is to help you create a better, stronger adhesion for your finished products to maintain your reputation and improve efficiencies within your production line.

Why Trust ThreeBond?

ThreeBond is more than just an adhesives manufacturer. We are a leader in sealants, coatings, and cleaners to ensure we can be a one-stop shop. As a reliable partner, we can help you find the solutions you need to improve production volume, prevent failures, and maintain a commitment to sustainability.

To be a full-service adhesive supplier, we offer high-quality formulas for liquid gaskets and adhesives, silicone RTV sealant, anaerobic adhesives, polymer adhesives, instant adhesives, and more. We are committed to providing top-quality adhesives solutions that accommodate every aspect of our client’s industries.

# TB1207B – SILICONE SEALANT

## Information

TB1207B is a high performance, room temperature vulcanized (RTV) silicone commonly used as a formed in place gasket (FIPG). TB1207B is characterized by good elasticity, temperature resistance and chemical resistance after curing.

# TB1217F – SILICONE SEALANT

## Information

TB1217F is a high performance, room temperature vulcanized (RTV) silicone commonly used as a formed in place gasket (FIPG). TB1217F is characterized by good elasticity, temperature resistance and chemical resistance after curing.

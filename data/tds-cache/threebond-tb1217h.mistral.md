# TB1217H – SILICONE SEALANT

## Information

TB1217H is a high performance, room temperature vulcanized (RTV) silicone commonly used as a formed in place gasket (FIPG). TB1217H has high elongation, low modulus, good elasticity, engine oil resistance (against SJ oil) and blowout resistance after curing.

Product: ThreeBond 1227D Silicone-based sealant. Technical Data, May 16, 2005, Three Bond Co., Ltd., document ED20090616.

Outline: dealcoholized type one-part cold-curing silicone-based sealant for FIPG use. It does not generate MEKO and is designed for engine oil and long-life coolant resistance. Suitable for oil-based and water-based liquids.

Features: good engine oil resistance; good long-life coolant resistance; does not generate MEKO; thermal and cold resistance from -60 C to 200 C; non-sag property.

Use: vehicle engine oil pans and chain cases.

Properties: appearance black; viscosity 200 Pa.s; specific gravity 1.46; tack-free time 90 min; thick-film curability 2.0 mm/day. Initial pressure resistance with air after 5 min, flange width 15 mm: 0.19 MPa at 0.2 mm clearance and >=0.40 MPa at 0.1 mm clearance.

Physical properties after 7 days at 25 C / 55% RH: hardness Shore A33; elongation 410%; tensile strength 2.3 MPa; tensile shear strength 2.2 MPa on aluminum/aluminum and 2.3 MPa on iron/iron.

Engine oil resistance after 150 C x100 h in Engine Oil 5W-20 SL: hardness Shore A14; elongation 380%; tensile strength 2.1 MPa; tensile shear strength 1.3 MPa on aluminum/aluminum and 0.6 MPa on iron/iron.

Long-life coolant resistance after 120 C x100 h in 50% diluted LLC: hardness Shore A33; elongation 360%; tensile strength 1.9 MPa; tensile shear strength 1.4 MPa on aluminum/aluminum and 1.4 MPa on iron/iron.

Storage: store at 10 to 25 C in a dark dry place avoiding direct sunlight. Cautions: this product is an insulating material, so avoid electric contacts; flammable; do not use for drinking water or hot-water supply piping; after applying, assemble parts as soon as possible.

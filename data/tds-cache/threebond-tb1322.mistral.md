# TB1322 - ANAEROBIC SEALANT

## Information

TB1322 is a high performance, red, and high strength anaerobic adhesive. It will not set so long as it is in contact with air. It cures rapidly once contacts to metal and in absence of air. Used mostly for locking bolts permanently.

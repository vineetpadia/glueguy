# TB1342 – ANAEROBIC SEALANT

## Information

TB1342 is a low strength, removable type thread locking adhesive. Since the TB1342 is an anaerobic adhesive, it will not set so long as it is in contact with air. TB1342 is characterized by consistent torque strength after heat and certain chemical exposure. It also hardens without shrinkage at normal temperatures.

## Application

Used for various adhesive-fastening applications where non-permanent bonding required.

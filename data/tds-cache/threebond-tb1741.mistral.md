# TB1741 CYANOACRYLATE ADHESIVE

## Information

TB1741 is a very low viscosity, fast curing, single component cyanoacrylate adhesive. It cures rapidly at room temperature and demonstrates excellent adhesive efficiency.

## Applications

TB1741 can be used mostly for bonding metals, plastics and rubber.

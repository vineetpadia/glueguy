# TB1742Z VERY FAST CURING CYANOACRYLATE ADHESIVE

## Information

TB1742Z is a low viscosity, very fast curing, single component cyanoacrylate adhesive. It cures rapidly at room temperature and demonstrates excellent adhesive efficiency.

## Applications

TB1742Z can be used mostly for bonding metals, plastics and rubbers.

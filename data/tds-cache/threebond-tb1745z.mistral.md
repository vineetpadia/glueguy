# TB1745Z VERY FAST CURING CYANOACRYLATE ADHESIVE

## Information

TB1745Z is a medium high viscosity, very fast curing, single component cyanoacrylate adhesive. It cures rapidly at room temperature and demonstrates excellent adhesive efficiency.

## Applications

TB1745Z can be used mostly for bonding metals, plastics and rubbers.

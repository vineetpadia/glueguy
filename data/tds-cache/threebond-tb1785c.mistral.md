# Three Bond International, Inc.
Technical Data Sheet

TB 1785C

## Information

TB1785C is an ethyl type, low viscosity, fast curing, and single component cyanoacrylate adhesive. When a thin layer of the adhesive is applied, the adhesive cures very rapidly and bonds various materials. It demonstrates excellent adhesive efficiency.

## Application

TB1785C can be used for a wide range of materials, especially for bonding metals, plastics and rubbers.

# Three Bond International, Inc.
## Technical Data Sheet

**TB Glue Accelerator 31**
**(TB Activator 1791A)**

## Information

Three Bond Glue Accelerator 31 is a clear to yellow liquid, which speeds up the cure rate of ThreeBond 1700 series instant adhesives.

## Application

TB Glue Accelerator 31 speeds up the cure rate of ThreeBond 1700 series instant adhesives. It helps create a strong bond quicker than instant adhesives used alone.

## Typical Properties

|  Properties | TB-TS | Value  |
| --- | --- | --- |
|  Appearance | 201-01 | Clear to yellowish liquid  |
|  Specific gravity (@ 25°C) | 213-02 | 0.84  |
|  Curing Time | - | 60 Sec. Max. with TB1747  |
|  Solid Content | 3Hrs.@105°C | 0.05%  |

These are typical results, not product specifications

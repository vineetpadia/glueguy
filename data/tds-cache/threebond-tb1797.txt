# Three Bond International, Inc.
## Technical Data Sheet

### TB 1797 (PP/PE Primer)

#### Information
Three Bond 1797 is a clear yellow liquid used exclusively as a primer for ThreeBond 1700 series instant adhesives.

#### Application
TB 1797 is used for bonding polypropylene, polyethylene polyacetal and EP rubber. It is also effective in applications to polyurethane and soft PVC.

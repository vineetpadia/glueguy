# Three Bond International, Inc.
Technical Data Sheet

TB 17N-040
HQ FREE 17A-078E

## Information

TB17N-040 is the HQ free version of 17A-078E. It is a high viscosity; single component cyanoacrylate adhesive. It cures rapidly at room temperature and demonstrates excellent adhesive efficiency.

## Application

Although TB17N-040 can be used for a wide range of materials, main application area is in cosmetics.

# TB17N-065 CYANOACRYLATE ADHESIVE

## Information

TB17N-065 is a low viscosity, fast curing, single component cyanoacrylate adhesive. It cures rapidly at room temperature and demonstrates excellent adhesive efficiency. TB17N-021 can be used mostly for bonding metals, plastics and rubbers.

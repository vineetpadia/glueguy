# TB2706 – MACHINE PARTS DEGREASING CLEANER

## Information
TB2706 is an aerosol-type powerful degreasing cleaner which quickly and easily removes build up of rust-preventive oil and grease by gas pressure and a chemical cleaner. Moreover, it has no corrosiveness on metals.

## Application
Used for degreasing cleaning of machine parts.

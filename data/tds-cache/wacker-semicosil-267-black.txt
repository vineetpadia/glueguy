# SEMICOSIL® 267 BLACK

SEMICOSIL®

Room Temperature Curing Silicone Rubber (RTV-2)

SEMICOSIL® 267 BLACK is a high shear-thinning, addition cure, one-part silicone rubber encapsulant / adhesive.

# Properties

- Ready to use, one-part
- Rheology optimized for high speed dispensing
- Thixotropic
- Low Bleed
- Rapid heat cure
- Excellent primerless adhesion to many substrates
- Solvent-free (100% solids)

# Application details

Specially recommended for local encapsulation and bonding of delicate electronic components.

Surface Preparation

The surface must be clean and free of contaminants that will inhibit the cure of SEMICOSIL® 267 BLACK. Example of contaminants are sulfur containing materials, plasticizers, urethanes, aminecontaing materilas and organometallic compounds, especially organotin compounds.

If a substrate's ability to inhibit cure is unknown, a small-scale test should be run to determine compatibility.

# Packaging and storage

## Storage

SEMICOSIL® 267 BLACK has a shelf life of 9 months form packaging date when stored between -40°C and -20°C in the original sealed container. The "Best used by" date appears on the product label. Storage beyond the date specified on the label does not necessarily meant that the product is no longer usable. In this case however, the properties required for the intended use must be checked for quality assurance.

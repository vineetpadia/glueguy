SEMICOSIL 9720 TC is a non-slump, thermally curable, addition-curing, one part silicone adhesive with excellent thermal conductivity.

Property Condition Value Method
Color - White
Density 23 C 2.74 g/cm3 DIN EN ISO 1183-1 A
Hardness Shore A 85 ASTM 2240
Dielectric strength 25 C 10 kV/mm IEC 60243
Thermal conductivity 1.9 W/m.K ASTM D 5470-12

Applications
- Bonding of electronic parts with heat sinks
- Fixing electronic parts to circuit boards
- Heat transmission glue for PCU modules

Storage
SEMICOSIL 9720 TC has a shelf life of at least 3 months when stored cool and dry in the originally sealed container.
